BU News 2024

New Data Science Lab Opens at Bangkok University

Bangkok University has inaugurated a state-of-the-art Data Science Lab to support its bachelor program in Data Science. The lab features cutting-edge hardware and software for big data analysis and machine learning projects.

Data Science Students Win National Hackathon

A team of undergraduate Data Science students from Bangkok University has won first place in the National Data Innovation Hackathon. Their project focused on using AI to optimize public transportation in Bangkok.

Industry Partnerships Boost Data Science Program

Bangkok University's Data Science program has partnered with leading tech companies to offer internships and real-world projects to students. This collaboration aims to bridge the gap between academia and industry needs.

Data Science Curriculum Updated for 2024

The Bachelor of Science in Data Science program at Bangkok University has undergone a major curriculum update. New courses in deep learning, natural language processing, and data ethics have been added to keep pace with industry trends.

Bangkok University Hosts International Data Science Conference

The university is set to host its first International Conference on Data Science and AI Applications next month. Renowned speakers from around the world will present cutting-edge research and industry applications.

Data Science Alumni Secure Top Positions in Tech Industry

Recent graduates from Bangkok University's Data Science program have been hired by leading tech companies, including Google, Amazon, and local unicorns. This success highlights the program's effectiveness in preparing students for the job market.

New Scholarship Program for Women in Data Science

Bangkok University announces a new scholarship program aimed at encouraging more women to pursue careers in Data Science. The program will cover full tuition and provide mentorship opportunities with successful women in the field.

Data Science Department Launches Community Outreach Program

In an effort to promote data literacy, the Data Science department at Bangkok University is launching a series of free workshops for local businesses and community organizations. These workshops will cover basic data analysis and visualization techniques.
